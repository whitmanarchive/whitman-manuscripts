• Title: Sands at Seventy
  • Creator: Walt Whitman
  • Date: Late 1880s
  • Whitman Archive ID: yal.00020
  • Source: Yale Collection of American Literature, Beinecke Rare Book and Manuscript Library. Transcribed from digital images of the original. For a description of the editorial rationale behind our treatment of the manuscripts, see our statement of editorial policy.
  • Editorial note: "Sands at Seventy" was the title of a collection of poems that first appeared in November Boughs (1888) and was later included as an "annex" to the final printings of Leaves of Grass. This manuscript was probably written in the late 1880s.
