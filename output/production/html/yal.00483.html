  [begin leaf 1 recto] 81. [cut away]
were paid for with a steamships or a [illegible], or
would come cheap.— I am not stuck up for these reasons; There is plenty of air left while I breathe and afterward. Because I am in my place what of that? The perfect male and female are everywhere in their place. The fly and the leaf of grass are in their place; The seen and unseen suns are in their place;   [begin leaf 1 verso]
