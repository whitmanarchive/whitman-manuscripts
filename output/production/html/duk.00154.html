Caution

☞ I to-day think it would be best not at all to bother with arguments against the foreign models, or to help American models—but just go on supplying American models Not to blaat constantly for Native American models, literature, &c, and ^cry bluster out "nothing foreign".—The best way to promulge Native American models and literature, is to supply ^such forcible and spuperb specimens of the same sathat they will, by their own volition, move to the head of all, and put foreign models in the second class.—   [begin leaf 1 verso]
