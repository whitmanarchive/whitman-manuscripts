  [begin leaf 1 recto] I shall venerate hours and days, [deletion,illegible] and think
them ^immeasurable infinite stupendous hereafter; I ^am finding how much I am passing can pass through
in a few minutes.— I was a ^good friend to all things before, But now what I was seems to me limpsy
and little.   [begin leaf 1 verso]
