  • Editorial note: Many of the items from this list of European rivers, lakes, and cities were included in "Poem of Salutation" in the 1856 edition of Leaves of Grass, suggesting that this manuscript was written between 1850 and 1856. At one point, this manuscipt likely formed part of Whitman's cultural geography scrapbook.
